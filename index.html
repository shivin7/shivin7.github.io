About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer at Pinterest, where I work on building large scale recommendation systems for related pins using machine learning techniques on the Close Up Recommendations team.

I completed my MS in Computational Linguistics at the University of Washington in 2022 and graduated from BITS Pilani in Computer Science and Mathematics in 2020. Previously, I have had the opportunity to work at Avnio, Samsung Research Institute, and complete my thesis at the IMS Lab at University of Stuttgart.
